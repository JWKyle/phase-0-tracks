My friends came over to try my mother's meatloaf. There were no survivors. I promise you, I warned them.

There are many reasons not to eat my mother's meatloaf. Victims may experience:

  • Nausea
  • Invisibility
  • Spontaneous Combustion

I've told her many times to modify her recipe. No one should consume anything that includes the following cooking process:

  1. Purchase ingredients from a mysterious store that sells Gremlins
  2. Marinate in magical liquid
  3. Cook
  4. Serve

If you would like to come to dinner, you may wish to purchase plenty of fire exstinguishers, just in case.
